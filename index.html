ABOUT US

Awareness is like the Sun. When it shines on things, they are transformed.

Learning Space Foundation strongly believes that the first step in creating a better society is empowering our future citizens. We work towards empowerment of marginalized children and youth by raising awareness and educating them about health, safety and social issues and building necessary skills to enable them overcome success barriers.

We work with other NGOs and create collaborative synergy by providing our services in program management, training the students, teachers and facilitating leadership workshops for organizational effectiveness.



OUR MISSION

To empower children and youth by building necessary skills that help them to be safe, happy and healthy. To develop, promote and encourage uplifting social and educational processes for students and teachers.
